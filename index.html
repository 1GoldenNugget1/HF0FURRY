Idea behind this callsign was participation in 2025 edition of Meme Appreciation Month this event which is dedicated to the younger part of our ham radio community (but actually anyone can join and gather some points and have some meme calls in their logbook). Starting from June 15 2025 we'll be on air transmitting some memes mainly over SSTV, FT-8 for whole month.
Legends says that we someone will activate this callsign in SSB.
